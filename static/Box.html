{CaravanHTML} Resource Finder
This helper scans a square around the world map starting from your selected city and filters out locations with Caravan-gather-able resources. This information is only updated when you click the Market Helper button at the top of this box.

The search automatically filters out resources that are:
  • occupied by armies
  • occupied by other gatherers
  • claimed by sovereignty
This filtering helps you stay "polite" with your gathering.

Every time you click the Send! button, the helper will dispatch a single caravan to that location. Sending a single caravan maximizes your gathering speed, lowers the chance of all your gatherers being "interrupted", and lowers the chance of over-committing caravans to a tiny patch of resources.
